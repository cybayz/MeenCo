Our Story

Born at Neendakara Harbor

Our journey began in the heart of Kollam, where fishing is more than a trade—it's a way of life. Inspired by the Neendakara Harbor, we set out to deliver the best catch, with care, tradition, and a promise of freshness.

Every fish we deliver carries the spirit of Kollam's fishing community—generations of knowledge, skill, and passion for the sea.

Kollam: The Fish Capital

The Heart of Kerala's Seafood Heritage

Kollam is known as the fish capital of Kerala, with the iconic Neendakara Harbor at its heart. For generations, this harbor has been a lifeline for Kollam's fishermen—bringing the freshest, most sought-after seafood to markets and homes across the region.
